The Exhibition

AI &Robotic technologies have made our lives easier in multiple ways. We can accessmultiple benefits right through our smartphones with the power of digital assistants like Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa, as well as smart machines andsocial robots. In today’s world, multiple industries such as healthcare, e-commerce, financial services, etc., are leveraging the benefits of AI to the fullest of its potential. The technology has helped businesses grow in leaps and bounds with improved quality, security, and efficiency.

Due to the complex challenges that the various industry face, the growth in the industryis severely limited. Since 2023, AIBotics as a conference has been brought closer to the local audiences. Together with our local partners, AIBotics has been collocated in Singapore, Thailand, Philippines, China and Korea. We believethat in this approach, we can bring an exchange of expertise closer to the local audience and motivate greater thought innovation and address more comprehensive issues locally.

Augmenting Human Potential

AI has the potential to significantly increase human capabilities in the following ways:

Providing Decision Support

AI can evaluate enormous datasets and offer useful insights, assisting people in making better decisions.

Improving Learning Experiences

AI-powered platforms provide individualized instruction tailored to each learner’s needs and preferences.

Enhancing Creativity

By generating ideas and prototypes, AI supports creative work, allowing humans to focus on higher-order thinking.

Increasing Productivity

Employees can dedicate more time to strategic initiatives by automating repetitive tasks with AI.
